Understanding how global time zones affect cryptocurrency trading volumes

The Trading Concept

🌙

Night Trading

When major Asian markets sleep, trading volumes typically decrease, potentially affecting prices

☀️

Day Trading

During Asian market hours, increased activity can lead to higher trading volumes

🤖

Automated Bots

Trading bots execute trades based on these time-based patterns

How the Strategy Works

Bots aim to buy during lower volume periods and sell during higher volume periods
